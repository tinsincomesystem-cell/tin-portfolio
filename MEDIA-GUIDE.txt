MEDIA STRUCTURE GUIDE

Premium agencies usually structure portfolios like this:

IMAGES FOR:
- funnels
- websites
- branding
- social graphics
- screenshots

VIDEOS FOR:
- walkthroughs
- systems demos
- automations
- SaaS previews

HOW TO CHANGE EACH MEDIA:
Open index.html and search:
<div class="sample-media">

Replace the content with either:

IMAGE:
<img src="assets/my-image.jpg">

OR VIDEO:
<video controls>
  <source src="assets/my-video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

Then place the file inside the assets folder.
